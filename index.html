ai_DELIVERY AT A GLANCE

ai _Delivery is a registered logistic company with a vision to provide earning opportunities to millions across the globe, especially in the developing countries, using blockchain technology to disrupt the logistic industry worldwide. First by powering $ZTNZ to facilitate seamless cross-border payments and innovating a Peer to Peer delivery system using a world class APP that connects or matches delivery request to anyone available to deliver such request and earn money. Also giving travellers the opportunity to take up delivery request from one geographical location to another, not to mention store keepers, traders, and eateries making home deliveries to whoever wants what anywhere. .

WHY ZTRANZIT TOKEN

$ZTNZ will be easily traded and converted to fiat. As peer to peer trading Section will be included on the platform. So, anyone can buy or sell $ZTNZ via bank transfer within their home countries.
